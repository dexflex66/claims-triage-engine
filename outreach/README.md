# Pilot acquisition pipeline (no existing clinic access)

2-week outbound sprint to land first pilots with mid-market radiology-heavy clinics in US and India.

## Steps

1. **List building**
   - US: Identify practices with 500+ imaging prior auths/year (AMA, state radiology societies, RCM vendor lists).
   - India: Identify hospitals/clinics with active cashless/TPA prior auth volume (Star, HDFC ERGO provider lists, hospital chains).

2. **Referral asks**
   - Radiologists: "Who in your network handles prior auth for your reads?"
   - RCM consultants: "Which 2–3 clients would pilot a denial-reduction tool?"

3. **Offer**
   - 15-minute workflow audit (no commitment).
   - Paid pilot: per-case fee first; performance pricing once baseline is measured.
   - Entry promise: reduce rework hours first, then improve approval rate.

4. **Metrics to capture from day 1**
   - Staff minutes per case, cases per reviewer per day.
   - Missing-doc rate before submit, resubmission rate.
   - Denial rate by payer and code, days-to-approval.

## Files in this folder

- `pilot_checklist.yaml`: week-by-week checklist.
- `outreach_copy.md`: short email/LinkedIn templates (no PHI).
