# Compliance and Legal Go-Live Checklist

## Regulatory and Contractual
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA) executed with each covered entity.
- Data Processing Addendum (DPA) executed for all subprocessors.
- Privacy notice and terms updated for claims data usage.
- Jurisdictional legal review completed for US and India processing paths.

## Security and Privacy
- PHI/PII data classification confirmed.
- Encryption in transit and at rest verified in production.
- Access control roles mapped to least privilege.
- Audit logging and retention windows approved by compliance.
- Incident response contacts and escalation paths confirmed.

## Operational Readiness
- UAT passed in live credential environment.
- Rollback drill completed and documented.
- Backup and restore verification completed.
- On-call runbook acknowledged by responsible engineer and manager.
